Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a hotel or tour?

Kashmir Stay is a directory platform. We list verified businesses with their contact information. To book a hotel or tour, contact the business directly using the phone, WhatsApp, or website link provided on their profile page.

How do I add my business to Kashmir Stay?

Visit MakeMe Top (our business portal) to create your business profile. After verification, your business will appear on Kashmir Stay for tourists to find.

How do I report a fraudulent or scam business?

Email us at report@kashmirstay.in with the business name, profile link, and details of the issue. We investigate all reports and take action against businesses violating our terms.

Can I get a refund from Kashmir Stay?

No. Kashmir Stay does not handle payments or bookings. All transactions are between you and the business. For refunds, contact the business directly.

How do I update my business listing?

Log in to your account at MakeMe Top and edit your business profile. Changes will reflect on Kashmir Stay within 24 hours.

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days (Monday-Saturday). Technical support may take longer depending on the complexity of the issue.
