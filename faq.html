About Kashmir Stay

What is Kashmir Stay?

Kashmir Stay is an online directory platform that lists verified hotels, resorts, houseboats, travel agencies, and tour operators in Kashmir. We help tourists find and connect with trusted businesses across Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, and all of Jammu & Kashmir.

Is Kashmir Stay a hotel booking platform?

No. We are NOT a booking platform. We are a directory that provides business contact information. All bookings, payments, and reservations are made directly between you and the business.

Does Kashmir Stay charge tourists?

No. Kashmir Stay is completely free for tourists. We do not charge booking fees, service fees, or commissions. Our revenue comes from business listings, not from tourists.

How are businesses verified?

We verify that businesses are legally registered with the Jammu & Kashmir Tourism Department, hold valid licenses, and comply with local regulations. However, verification does not guarantee service quality — always read reviews and contact businesses before booking.

Booking & Payments

How do I book a hotel or tour?

Contact the business directly using the phone number, WhatsApp, email, or website link provided on their profile. All bookings are made directly with the business, not through Kashmir Stay.

Can I make payments through Kashmir Stay?

No. Kashmir Stay does not handle any payments. All payments are made directly to the business you're booking with.

What if a business asks for advance payment?

Many Kashmir businesses ask for advance payments to confirm bookings. Always get written confirmation (WhatsApp, email) with payment receipt, booking details, and cancellation policy before making any payment.

Can I get a refund through Kashmir Stay?

No. Kashmir Stay does not handle refunds. For cancellations and refunds, contact the business directly. Check their cancellation policy before booking.

Traveling to Kashmir

Is Kashmir safe for tourists?

Yes, Kashmir is generally safe for tourists. However, always check current travel advisories, avoid restricted areas near the Line of Control (LoC), and follow local authorities' instructions. Read our Kashmir Travel Guide for detailed safety tips.

Do I need special permits to visit Kashmir?

For most tourists: No. Indian citizens can visit Srinagar, Gulmarg, Pahalgam, and Sonamarg without permits. However, remote areas like Gurez Valley require Inner Line Permits (ILP). Foreign tourists may need additional permissions. Check with local authorities before traveling.

What is the best time to visit Kashmir?

Summer (April-June): Pleasant weather, ideal for sightseeing and trekking.
Winter (December-February): Heavy snowfall, perfect for skiing in Gulmarg.
Autumn (September-November): Clear skies, golden meadows, less crowded.
Avoid July-August (monsoon season).

How do I reach Kashmir?

By Air: Srinagar International Airport (SXR) is well-connected to major Indian cities.
By Train: Nearest railway station is Jammu Tawi (290 km from Srinagar).
By Road: National Highway 44 connects Jammu to Srinagar (8-9 hours).

Using Kashmir Stay

How do I search for hotels or travel agencies?

Use the search bar on our homepage, browse by category (Hotel, Resort, Travel Agency), or filter by location. Each business has a detailed profile with contact information and customer reviews.

How do I know if a business is trustworthy?

Look for:
✅ Verified badge (government-registered)
✅ Customer reviews and ratings
✅ Complete business information
✅ Active phone numbers and website
Always read reviews and contact the business before making any commitments.

Can I leave a review?

Yes! After your trip, visit the business profile on Kashmir Stay and submit a review. Your feedback helps other tourists make informed decisions.

What if I find incorrect information on a listing?

Report it to us at report@kashmirstay.in with the business name and details. We'll investigate and update the listing.

For Business Owners

How do I add my business to Kashmir Stay?

Visit MakeMe Top (our business portal) to create your profile. After verification, your business will appear on Kashmir Stay for tourists to find.

Is there a fee to list my business?

Basic listings are free. Premium listings with priority placement and additional features are available for a fee. Visit MakeMe Top for pricing.

How long does verification take?

Typically 2-3 business days after you submit all required documents.

Problems & Support

What if a business scams me?

Report it immediately to report@kashmirstay.in with evidence (screenshots, receipts). We'll investigate and may remove the business from our platform. Also file a complaint with local tourism authorities and police.

What if a business doesn't respond?

Try contacting them via alternative methods (WhatsApp instead of phone, email instead of call). If they remain unresponsive, try another business from Kashmir Stay.

Can Kashmir Stay help resolve disputes?

No. Kashmir Stay is not involved in transactions between tourists and businesses. Disputes must be resolved directly with the business or through legal channels.
