Gulmarg Complete Travel Guide 2026

Skiing, Gondola Rides, Best Time to Visit & More

Gulmarg, meaning "Meadow of Flowers," is Kashmir's premier ski resort and one of India's most beautiful hill stations. Located 51 km from Srinagar at an altitude of 2,650 meters, Gulmarg transforms into a winter wonderland from December to March, attracting skiers and snowboarders from around the world.

Best Time to Visit Gulmarg

Winter (December to March) – For Snow & Skiing

This is peak season for Gulmarg. Heavy snowfall blankets the entire valley, creating perfect conditions for skiing, snowboarding, and snow activities. The Gulmarg Gondola operates at full capacity, taking you to Apharwat Peak at 4,200 meters. Temperatures range from -8°C to 5°C.

🎿 Best for: Skiing, snowboarding, sledding, Gondola rides to snow-covered peaks, and experiencing a white Christmas in Kashmir.

Summer (April to June) – For Trekking & Greenery

From April onwards, snow melts and Gulmarg's meadows bloom with wildflowers. This season is ideal for trekking, golfing at Asia's highest golf course, and enjoying pleasant weather. Temperatures range from 10°C to 25°C. The Gondola runs smoothly and offers panoramic views of the Himalayas.

Autumn (September to November)

Post-monsoon, Gulmarg is less crowded, with cool weather and clear skies perfect for photography. Light snowfall begins in late November.

⚠️ Avoid: July-August (monsoon season) – Gondola may be closed due to weather, and heavy rains reduce visibility.

Gulmarg Gondola – Asia's Highest Cable Car

The Gulmarg Gondola is the main attraction, taking visitors from Gulmarg base (2,600m) to Kongdoori Station (3,080m) in Phase 1, and then to Apharwat Peak (3,979m) in Phase 2.

Gondola Ticket Prices (2026)

  • Phase 1 (Base to Kongdoori): ₹740 per person (Round trip)
  • Phase 2 (Kongdoori to Apharwat): ₹950 per person (Round trip)
  • Full Journey (Both Phases): ₹1,690 per person
  • Children (under 12): 50% discount
💡 Pro Tip: Book Gondola tickets early in the morning (8-9 AM) to avoid long queues, especially in peak winter season. Carry an ID proof for ticket purchase.

Skiing & Snowboarding in Gulmarg

Gulmarg offers skiing for all levels – from beginners to advanced. The ski season runs from late December to early March.

Ski Packages & Rentals

  • Beginner Ski Package: ₹2,500 - ₹3,500/day (includes equipment rental, instructor, lift pass)
  • Equipment Rental Only: ₹1,200 - ₹1,800/day (skis, boots, poles)
  • Snowboard Rental: ₹1,500 - ₹2,000/day
  • Private Ski Instructor: ₹2,000 - ₹4,000/day

Multiple ski schools operate in Gulmarg, including Kashmir Alpine Ski School and Indian Institute of Skiing & Mountaineering (IISM).

  • Heevan Retreat: ₹8,000 - ₹15,000/night

Mid-Range Hotels

  • Hotel Affarwat: ₹2,500 - ₹5,000/night

Budget Stays

🏨 Booking Tip: Book hotels 2-3 months in advance for winter season (Dec-Feb). Prices double during peak weeks like Christmas and New Year.

How to Reach Gulmarg

From Srinagar (51 km, 1.5 hours)

  • Taxi: ₹2,000 - ₹3,000 one-way (shared taxis ₹400-500 per seat)
  • Bus: JKSRTC buses from TRC Srinagar – ₹100-150 per person

From Srinagar Airport

Srinagar International Airport is the nearest airport (56 km). Direct taxis to Gulmarg cost ₹2,500 - ₹3,500.

From Jammu (290 km, 8-9 hours)

Jammu Tawi is the nearest railway station. Hire a taxi (₹6,000 - ₹8,000) or take a bus to Srinagar first, then onward to Gulmarg.

Things to Do in Gulmarg

  1. Gondola Ride to Apharwat Peak: Stunning 360° views of snow-capped peaks
  2. Skiing & Snowboarding: India's best ski slopes for all levels
  3. Gulmarg Golf Course: World's highest 18-hole golf course (summer only)
  4. Sledding & Snowman Building: Fun snow activities for families
  5. Trekking: Summer treks to Alpather Lake and Khilanmarg
  6. Snow Scooter Rides: ₹500 - ₹1,000 for short rides
  7. Photography: Capture sunrise/sunset over Nanga Parbat

What to Pack for Gulmarg

Winter (Dec-Mar)

  • Heavy woolens, thermal wear, waterproof jackets
  • Snow boots with good grip
  • Gloves, woolen cap, sunglasses (snow glare)
  • Moisturizer, lip balm (dry cold)

Summer (Apr-Jun)

  • Light woolens, windproof jacket
  • Comfortable trekking shoes
  • Sunscreen, hat

Travel Tips for Gulmarg

  • Cash: ATMs are limited in Gulmarg. Carry sufficient cash from Srinagar.
  • Altitude: Acclimatize for a day in Srinagar before going to Gulmarg to avoid altitude sickness.
  • Mobile Network: BSNL and Jio work best. Airtel/Vi have limited coverage.
  • Permits: No special permits required for Indian tourists. Foreign tourists need to register with local police.
  • Food: Most hotels have restaurants. Try local Kashmiri Wazwan cuisine.

Gulmarg Itinerary Suggestion

Day 1: Arrival & Acclimatization

Arrive in Gulmarg from Srinagar. Check into hotel. Explore Gulmarg market. Light walk around meadows. Early dinner and rest.

Day 2: Gondola & Snow Activities

Morning Gondola ride to Apharwat Peak (both phases). Photography, snow play. Afternoon skiing or sledding. Return by 5 PM.

Day 3: Departure

Morning visit to St. Mary's Church or golf course. Departure to Srinagar by noon.

📸 Instagram Tip: Best photo spots – Gondola Phase 2 platform, sunset at Apharwat, and meadows with pine trees in foreground.

Conclusion

Gulmarg is a must-visit destination for anyone traveling to Kashmir. Whether you're a skiing enthusiast, a nature lover, or simply seeking a peaceful mountain retreat, Gulmarg offers unforgettable experiences year-round. Plan your trip during winter for skiing or summer for trekking and greenery.
